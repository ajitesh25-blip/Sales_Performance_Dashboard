- Sales Performance Dashboard — Power BI

- Project Overview
The "Sales Performance Dashboard" is an interactive Power BI solution designed to help businesses track and analyze their sales activities across  quantity,products, and customer segments.  
It provides a complete overview of sales KPIs such as "Sales", "Profit" and "Quantity" .

This dashboard helps decision-makers quickly identify trends, strengths, and areas that need improvement.

---

- Key Objectives
- Analyze regional and category-wise sales performance  
- Track revenue and profit trends over time  
- Identify top- and bottom-performing products  
- Compare yearly sales performance  
- Provide actionable insights for strategic decision-making  

---

- Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards  
- Total Sales  
- Total Profit  
- Profit Margin  
 

- Visualizations  
- Sales by   
- Sales by Product Category   
- Sales by Customer Segment  

- Slicers 
- Year 
- Product Category   
- Customer Segment
